## ISO/IEC 27001

- ISO/IEC 27001 is the leading international standard for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an _information security management system (ISMS)_ What is an ISM


- it is jointly published by:
    - ISO - international organization for standardization
    - IEC - international electrotechnical commission

- current version : _ISO/IEC 27001:2022_

## What is an ISMS

- _information security management system (ISMS)_ is a structured framework of:
    - Policies
    - Procedures
    - Processes
    - Controls
    - People & technology
- its purpose is to protect
    - _confidentiality_ - information is accessible only to authorized persons
    - _integrity_ - information is accurate and protected from unauthorized modification
    - _availability_ - information is accessible when needed

## why ISO 27001 Exists

- organizations face risks such as:
    - data breaches
    - ransomware attacks
    - insider threats
    - system failures
    - regulatory penalties
- _ISO 27001_ provides a risk-based approach to managing these threats systematically rather than reacting to incidents

## structure of ISO/IEC 27001:2022

- the standard follows the _Annex SL High-Level Structure_, making it compatible with other ISO standards like ISO 9001 and ISO 14001

- clauses (1-10) 

| clause | Description                        |
|--------|------------------------------------|
| 1-3    | scope, normatives reference, Terms |
| 4      | context of the organization        |
| 5      | leadership                         |
| 6      | planning                           |
| 7      | support                            |
| 8      | operation                          |
| 9      | performance evaluation             |
| 10     | improvement                        |

- _plan -> do -> check -> act_

- cloud service providers
- financial institutions
