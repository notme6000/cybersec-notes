# SATURDAY MASTER PLAN (6–7 Effective Hours)

We divide Saturday into 4 blocks.

## Block 1 (Morning – 2 hrs)

Deep Technical Practice

* Advanced topic from week
* 50–60 Professional Knowledge MCQs
* Analyze mistakes

## Block 2 (Midday – 1.5 hrs)

Quant + Reasoning Intensive

* 1 sectional timed practice (30–40 mins)
* 1 hr mistake analysis

## Block 3 (Evening – 1.5 hrs)

Banking & IT Awareness

Study:

* Digital banking systems
* NPCI, UPI, RTGS, NEFT
* RBI cybersecurity guidelines
* Basic financial awareness

This helps both exam + interview.

## Block 4 (Night – 1 hr)

Revision + Short Notes

* Revise entire week topics
* Update formula sheet
* Update cybersecurity concept sheet

Revision is what converts effort into selection.
